This app only stores information internally, no information leaves the app.

Information that is stored is:
  - The status of the most reason unfinished game.
  - Highscore with name/tag of your choice, points achieved and time needed..

The highscores can be deleted by pressing for more than 10 seconds on the name of the solitaire for which you want to delete the highscores.

The data will not be used for any other purpose than to display the highscore in the app.
